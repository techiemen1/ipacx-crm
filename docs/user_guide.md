# User Guide: Enterprise ERP System

## 1. CRM & Sales

### 1.1 Managing Leads
- **Navigate**: **CRM > Board** or **Profiles > Clients**.
- **Add Lead**: Click **Add Lead**. Enter Name, Phone, and Status (Lead/Prospect).
- **Communication**:
  - In the **Client List**, look for the "Connect" column.
  - Click the **WhatsApp** icon to open WhatsApp Web.
  - Click the **Mail** icon to open your email client.

### 1.2 Sales Pipeline
- **Deals**: A "Lead" becomes a "Deal" when there is a potential sale.
- **Kanban Board**: Drag and drop deals from **New** -> **Qualified** -> **Closed Won**.
- **Actions**: Click on a card to Edit, Add Notes, or see Contact Info.

### 1.3 Bulk Actions
- Go to **Profiles > Clients** or toggle **List View** in CRM.
- Select multiple records using the checkboxes.
- A toolbar appears at the top. Click **Email** to BCC all selected clients, or **Delete** to remove them.

## 2. Accounts & Invoicing

### 2.1 Creating an Invoice
- Navigate: **Accounts > Invoices > New Invoice**.
- **Select Customer**: If the customer has a GSTIN, the system auto-calculates taxes.
- **Place of Supply**: Ensure this is correct. Default is the customer's state.
  - **Intra-State**: CGST + SGST applied automatically.
  - **Inter-State**: IGST applied automatically.
- **Items**: Add items. Tax is calculated per item.

### 2.2 Payment Recording
- Open an Invoice -> Click **Record Payment**.
- Enter Amount and Method (Cheque, NEFT). This updates the Invoice status to "Paid" or "Partial".

## 3. Human Resources (Staff)

### 3.1 Attendance
- Login as Staff.
- Go to **HR**.
- Click **Mark Attendance** (if enabled) or view your monthly summary.

### 3.2 View Profile
- Go to **Settings > My Profile**.
- You can view your Designation, Department, and Salary information here.
